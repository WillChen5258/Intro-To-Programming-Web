About Me

Click to see my picture!

Will Chen is a technical sound designer, audio post-production professional, and audio engineer
specializing in immersive sound for film, games, and virtual experiences. Classically trained as a
violinist since age five, he has performed with ensembles such as the Richmond Delta Youth
Orchestra and the Berklee Motion Picture Orchestra, bringing a blend of musicality and technical
precision to his work.

His credits include collaborating with Columbia University directors on the short film Close and
working with teams from Berklee, MIT, and Harvard on VR game experiences showcased at MIT’s
Brain and Cognitive Sciences Complex. Experienced in sound design, audio implementation,
producing, recording, and mixing in surround and Dolby Atmos, Will focuses on creating audio that
enhances storytelling, deepens emotional impact, and pushes the boundaries of spatial and
immersive sound.
